# Seedance 2 Prompt Guide

## Best For

- Cinematic product shots, fashion spots, character motion, and short scene beats.
- Image-to-video prompts where subject continuity matters.
- Prompts that benefit from direct camera and motion language.

## Prompt Shape

```text
Subject:
Scene:
Camera:
Motion:
Lighting:
Mood:
Constraints:
```

## Good Prompt Example

```text
A matte black espresso machine sits on a pale marble counter in a quiet morning kitchen. Slow cinematic push-in from a three-quarter angle, shallow depth of field, soft window light, gentle steam rising from the cup, subtle reflections on brushed metal, premium product-commercial mood. Keep the machine shape stable and avoid extra objects appearing.
```

## Notes

- Be explicit about camera movement.
- Describe what should stay stable across the shot.
- Put product, character, and scene continuity before style words.
